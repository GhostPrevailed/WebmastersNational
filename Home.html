What is Green Energy?

Green energy, also reffered to as Renewable energy, is energy sourced from resources that are available naturally and quickly in nature. This is contrast to fossil fuels which aren't infinite like green energy and they also cause a lot of harm to the environment, unlike green energy. Examples of green energy can include solar power, wind power, hydroelectricity and more. These sources are always going to be available in nature and they cause significantly less harm to nature than fossil fuels. Green energy is the most important factor in fighting back climate change, which is
heavily caused by fossil fuel extraction.
