Now, Let's talk about specific practices and their money savings.

Solar Panels

Adding Solar Panels cost $3 per watt on average. This might sound a lot but, on average, people save around $1000 per year by using solar panels. Not just in this way,using Solar Panels also increase your chances of getting a tax rebate that results in a 30% deduction of the installation fee of the solar panel. Check your eligibility as well!

Wind Energy Systems

Wind Energy systems cost about $5 per 120 kilowatts. Installation ranges from $10,000 to $70,000 and this too has the chance of the 30% tax rebate. Individuals, on average, save around $300 per year using this clean energy source.

Solar Water Heating

Although, this is a very uncommon clean energy alternative that regular people use. This too can be beneficial for certain individuals. Solar Water Heating systems cost around $5000-$7000 to install and of course, they too come under the same 30% tax rebate.
